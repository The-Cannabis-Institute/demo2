South Africa’s online training Cannabis Institute that offers SAQA aligned/accredited Cannabis courses covering ALL areas of the industry.

Our offerings are SAQA aligned/accredited, NQF aligned skills programmes for future occupations and trades in the new Cannabis sector as it evolves with government legislation and global trends.

We use global trends that identify competencies that are universal and appropriate for all.

The goal is to be an innovative leading service provider with a defined focus on meeting industry needs both in the medical and farming sector through a creative and integrated approach.

We believe that focused, industry-led education is the key to addressing South Africa’s crippling unemployment problem. Our approach is to make learning accessible to all South Africans via our e-learning platform, to industrialise and commercialise cannabis whilst unlocking economic opportunities for all South Africans.

We are a fully online institute which aims to prepare students for the highly lucrative and ever-growing global cannabis sector. Our courses are all prepared by industry professionals and academics in a joint mission to bridge the gaps in the industry. Our mission is to strive to promote a symbiotic relationship with industry by providing skilled and competent graduates.
